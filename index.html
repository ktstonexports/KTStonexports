About Us

KT Stone Export Co. has been doing successful jobs in different verticals of construction business. Initially since 2002 it has been involved in supply of natural stone, marble and granite exclusively from India, Italy and China. Its prime area of focus has been supply to major contractors and sub-contractors already established in GCC market.
